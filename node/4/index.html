About The Show

The Dan & Joe Show is, at this point, just Dan & Joe, two friends from the Minneapolis/St. Paul area, hanging out and having fun. From time to time, we get together and talk about music, movies or just whatever we want to talk about.

Also, we are a fan of podcasts, and we're curious what happens when we put one out there. Plus, it's the fun of making a Drupal web site to do all these things and get it working.
